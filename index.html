Transience

Concepts

Let's begin with the concept of transcience. When you are transcient, you:

   1. Keep your existing momentum.

   2. Not affected by worldy forces such as Gravity and Friction


You can also use the builtin level editor to make your own levels and share them with the world!


Protip #1: Avoid the green aliens.

Protip #2: If you stop phasing behind a block, you'll be pushed to where you started phasing.

Happy phasing! - ♥ from the Transience team
