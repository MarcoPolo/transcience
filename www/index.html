Transcience

Concepts

Let's begin with the concept of transcience. When you are transcient, you:

1. Phase into a different dimension.

2. Maintain the velocity you had in the previous dimension.


So, when you phase, your interactions with objects in this dimension are different and your your velocity is not affected by different forces. To help get you started, when you phase, you can go through certain objects and you can go further if you're currently moving. Can you think of ways to complete a level with those two ideas in mind?
